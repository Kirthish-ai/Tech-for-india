About Us

Building a movement of leaders to eliminate educational inequity in India

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To eliminate educational inequity in India by building a movement of leaders who will work to ensure that all children attain an excellent education.

Vision

One day all children will attain an excellent education. We believe this is possible if we work together as a movement of leaders.

Our Approach

Teaching Fellowship

Two-year leadership development program for young professionals to teach in low-income schools.

Alumni Network

Building a network of leaders working across sectors to create systemic change.

Community Engagement

Working with parents, schools, and communities to create sustainable change.

Learn More About Our Work

Our Values

Love

We believe in the power of love to transform lives and communities.

Equity

We are committed to ensuring that every child has access to an excellent education.

Partnership

We work in partnership with communities, schools, and organizations to achieve our mission.

Innovation

We continuously innovate and learn to improve our impact.
